Services & Pricing

Straightforward pricing.
No surprises.

Pick a service, pay securely online, and you'll be taken straight to a page to upload your files — no waiting on an email back and forth.

Mixing

$150/ song

For stems that need to be balanced, shaped, and brought to life before mastering.

  • Up to 48 tracked stems
  • Level balancing, EQ & compression
  • Spatial depth & stereo width
  • 2 free revision rounds
  • 3–5 business day turnaround

Mastering

$80/ song

The final pass — loudness, tonal balance, and translation across every system.

  • Commercial loudness targets
  • Multi-system reference checks
  • Stereo enhancement & limiting
  • 2 free revision rounds
  • 24–48 hour turnaround

Vocal Production

$120/ song

Tuning, comping, and processing to bring a vocal to the front of the mix.

  • Pitch correction & timing
  • Comping from multiple takes
  • De-essing & vocal chain processing
  • 2 free revision rounds
  • 3–5 business day turnaround
ⓘ Payments are processed securely through Stripe. After checkout, you'll be redirected to a page to upload your project files and add any notes for the engineer.
